Forever~ Forever is an incomplete truth. Nothing is forever until it's not bound.
The trees stand forever until they are bound by ground. The seas are forever because they are bound by nature.
The north and south pole are together since they are bound by attractions. Eternity is comforting myth.
Forever is truth till you want it to be true. Donot create a self-illusion with someone or something.
So next time you say forever, ask yourself are you bound by it?
